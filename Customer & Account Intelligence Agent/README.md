# Customer & Account Intelligence Agent

This project is a lightweight AI-assisted tool designed to simulate how Product, Customer Success, and GTM teams evaluate and prioritize customer accounts.

The goal is to turn external signals and company-level data into clear, structured insights that help teams decide where to focus and what actions to take.

### Overview

Teams often have limited time and many accounts to manage. This tool helps prioritize accounts by analyzing:

- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) fit  
- Business signals (growth, hiring, product expansion)  
- Risks and barriers  
- Opportunities for engagement  
- Recommended next actions  

It is designed to mirror real workflows in:
- GTM Operations  
- Customer Success  
- Product Support (for high-value accounts)  

---

### Input

- Company name or customer account  
- Optional: industry or notes  

Example:
- Spotify  

---

### Output

Structured account report including:

- ICP Fit (High, Medium, Low)  
- Key Signals (funding, hiring, product direction)  
- Risks (sales friction, internal constraints)  
- Opportunities (expansion areas, use cases)  
- Priority Score (1–5)  
- Recommended Next Action  
- Customer Experience Implications  

---

### Process

1. Input a company or account name  
2. Use a structured prompt in Claude to analyze the account  
3. Generate categorized insights (signals, risks, opportunities)  
4. Translate insights into clear, actionable recommendations  
5. Format output for easy decision-making

### Example

See: `Spotify — Account Intelligence Report`

This example demonstrates how the tool:
- identifies strategic signals (profitability, expansion into new verticals)  
- evaluates risk (build vs buy culture, long sales cycles)

### Key Takeaways

This project demonstrates my ability to:

- Translate unstructured information into actionable insights  
- Think in terms of prioritization and tradeoffs  
- Connect business signals to customer and product implications  
- Support decision-making across GTM, Customer Success, and Support teams
- Frame account analysis in terms of revenue potential, sales cycle risk, and expansion opportunity — not just operational fit.

The focus is not just analysis, but helping teams decide **where to focus and how to act**.
