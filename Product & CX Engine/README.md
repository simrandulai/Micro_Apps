# Product & CX Engine

This project is an AI-assisted tool designed to transform raw customer support tickets into structured insights that drive product improvements, reduce support volume, and improve the overall customer experience.

The goal is to simulate how high-performing Customer Success, Product, and CX Operations teams identify patterns, diagnose root causes, and prioritize what to fix.

### Overview

Customer support data is one of the most valuable but underutilized sources of product insight.

This tool helps teams:

- Identify recurring customer issues  
- Understand root causes behind support tickets  
- Prioritize product and UX improvements  
- Reduce ticket volume through proactive fixes  

It mirrors real workflows in:

- Customer Support & CX Operations  
- Product Operations  
- Customer Success  
- VoC (Voice of Customer) analysis  

---

### Input

Two input methods:

**1. Manual Entry**
- Individual tickets entered with:
  - Description  
  - Journey stage (Onboarding, Active, Retention, etc.)  
  - Severity  

**2. Bulk Input**
- Paste ticket data from spreadsheets or CSV  
- One ticket per line  

**Example inputs:**
- “Gmail integration keeps disconnecting”  
- “Not sure how to start a campaign”  
- “Dashboard is slow and confusing”  

---

### Output

The tool generates a structured **Support & CX Insights Report** including:

- Theme Clusters (grouped issues)  
- Root Causes (product, UX, or process gaps)  
- Product Recommendations  
- Support Improvements (docs, macros, workflows)  
- Impact Estimates (% ticket reduction)  
- Top 2 High-Impact Opportunities  
- Journey Stage Breakdown (where users struggle most)  

---

### Process

1. Input support tickets or user feedback  
2. Group tickets into themes based on similarity  
3. Identify underlying root causes  
4. Translate issues into:
   - Product improvements  
   - Support process fixes  
5. Estimate impact on ticket volume and experience  
6. Highlight highest-priority opportunities
